Understanding Panchang

What is Tithi?

Tithi is a lunar day in the Hindu calendar. It represents the angular relationship between the Sun and Moon. There are 30 tithis in a lunar month, each lasting approximately 19-26 hours.

Understanding Paksha

Shukla Paksha: The bright half of the lunar month (waxing moon)
Krishna Paksha: The dark half of the lunar month (waning moon)

Panchang Elements

Vara: Day of the week
Nakshatra: Lunar constellation
Deity: Ruling deity of the day
Muhurat: Auspicious timing

About Hindu Panchang Calendar

The Hindu Panchang Calendar is an ancient vedic calendar system that provides comprehensive information about lunar days, festivals, and auspicious timings. This web application brings traditional panchang wisdom to the digital age.

Features:

  • Daily Tithi and Paksha information
  • Major Hindu festival dates
  • Auspicious timing (Muhurat)
  • Nakshatra and ruling deity details
  • Interactive calendar view
