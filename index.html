🌙 What is Tithi?

Tithi is a lunar day in the Hindu calendar. There are 30 tithis in a lunar month - 15 in Shukla Paksha (waxing moon) and 15 in Krishna Paksha (waning moon).

🕐 What is Muhurat?

Muhurat is an auspicious time period for important events like weddings, starting a business, or housewarming.
